<threat_model>
## Threat Model

| Threat | Severity | Mitigation |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Duplicate provider results create conflicting paper entities | high | Merge by DOI, arXiv ID, OpenAlex ID, Semantic Scholar ID, URL, then normalized title/authors. |
| A blog/news/vendor page is selected as ground truth | high | Selection rejects or supplements all `CandidateKind.non_paper` candidates. |
| No evidence found is overstated as proof no paper exists | medium | Use explicit wording `no scientific evidence found for now` and status `no_paper_found`. |
| Search provenance is lost after merge | medium | Preserve all discovery paths and merge keys on merged candidates. |
</threat_model>
