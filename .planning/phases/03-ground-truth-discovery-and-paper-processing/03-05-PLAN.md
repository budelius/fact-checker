<threat_model>
## Threat Model

| Threat | Severity | Mitigation |
|--------|----------|------------|
| LLM follows instructions embedded in paper text | high | Wrap paper text with `wrap_untrusted_text` before summarization prompts. |
| Markdown, MongoDB, and Qdrant drift immediately | high | Build all records from the same `PaperMetadata`, `PaperChunk`, and `PaperSummary` objects and assert matching vault paths/UUIDs in tests. |
| Duplicate authors/papers are created on rerun | high | Upsert by stable UUIDs and provide deterministic UUID helper or dedupe mapping based on external IDs before persistence. |
| Qdrant vectors are created with wrong trace payload | medium | Tests assert `source_uuid`, `chunk_id`, `vault_path`, and relationship UUIDs are present. |
</threat_model>
